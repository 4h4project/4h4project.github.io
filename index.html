About
RIBHA

The RIBHA Savings & Credit Cooperative Society (RSCCS) is a registered, Shari'a-compliant financial platform, called "The Society", comprising of Muslim members working together as a team to assist one another to achieve their economic aspirations.

Objectives

The broad objective of the society is to encourage the culture of savings among members, promote their ecomomic interest by assisting them (through loan and advice) to embark on profitable ventures that will improve their living standards.

Membership

The membership of the society consists of individual (males & females) that are not less than 18 years. Trustee can register a minor until such minor attains the age of 18 years before taking over his/her membership status. Membership is also also opened to registered Islamic organisations. Interested individual/organisation shall pay a non-refundable entrance fee of N2,500 only.

Members shall own shares and make contributions as may be specified to retain his/her membership of the society. A member wishimg to withdraw from the society for any reason will recieve his/her total contributions, i.e (savings + share deposit and /or dividend accrued thereform).

Savings/Deposits

Savings Contributions: Savings contribution is compulsory for each member to retain his/her membership to the society. Contributions by members shall be on a daily basis, payable every fortnight or monthly. Minimum amount of daily contribution is N200.00, which is subject to periodic review.

Share Deposit (SD): All members shall be allocated compulsory shares,payable once or on installment. Each shares shall be valued as agreed by the society which is subject to review from time to time based on performance.

Special Deposit Account (SDA): These are deposits by members other than savings contribution which can be withdrawn at any time after giving 5 working days notice of withdrawal. Withdrawal from this account shall have preference over other deposits and loan applications. The balance on the account cannot be used for loan request.

Target Deposit Acount (TDA): These are deposits by members other then savings contribution and SDA for a specific purpose or period of time. Where member elect to use the TDA for investment purposes, such a member shall be entitled to a portion of profit generated. Target depositors whose funds are withdrawn before the due date shall be entitled to thier deposit amount only.

Loans

Members shall be eligible for interest-free loan of twice their contribution balance after six(6) months of active participation in the society activities. Applicants for loans must have fully paid off the value of alloted shares and other stipulated conditions to qualify for loan.

Meetings

The Society holds General Meetings every fortnight. There are other meetings sush as Extra-ordinary, General meetings, and Annual General Meetings. Participation in all meetings and activities of the society is compulsory for all members.

Administration

The society shall be administered by a management committee to be elected by members at the AGM. The management committee shall derive its power and functions from the Bye-laws of the society which is governed by the Nigerian Cooperative Societies Act.
